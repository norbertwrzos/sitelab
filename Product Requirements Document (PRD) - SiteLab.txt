1. Executive Summary

SiteLab is a website design, creation, and deployment company offering a unique, modern web presence for entrepreneurs and business owners. The core differentiator is a 24-hour demo preview system that allows prospects to see a customized website draft before committing, while simultaneously enabling structured lead collection.

The goal is to eliminate the complicated, slow, and technical process of building a fully functional business website and replace it with a fast, frictionless experience.

2. Product Vision

To become the fastest and most accessible way for entrepreneurs and businesses to get a professional, high-converting website without technical complexity.
